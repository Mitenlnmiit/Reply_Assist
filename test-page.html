Chat Refinement Assistant - Test Page

How to Test the Extension:

  1. Make sure the Chat Refinement Assistant extension is installed and enabled
  2. Configure your Gemini API key in the extension settings
  3. Type a message in the input field below
  4. Press Ctrl+Shift+P to preview a refined version
  5. Press Ctrl+Shift+L to replace with refined version

Sample Conversation

Alice: Hey! How was your day? I heard you had that big presentation today.
You: It went okay, thanks for asking! The presentation was a bit nerve-wracking but I think it went well overall.
Alice: That's great to hear! I'm sure you did amazing. What was the presentation about?
You: It was about our new product launch strategy. We covered market analysis, target demographics, and our go-to-market plan.
Alice: Sounds interesting! I'd love to hear more about it sometime. Are you free for coffee this weekend?
